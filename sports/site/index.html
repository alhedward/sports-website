Why this exists

Whatever your situation, there is probably a sport-shaped door you can open.

This site points people away from dead-end search results and towards official sporting bodies, inclusive participation resources, local pathways, and major events that can spark interest.

Not just elite sport

We include pathways for playing, spectating, volunteering, coaching, officiating, disability sport, juniors, adults, social formats, and club administrators.
