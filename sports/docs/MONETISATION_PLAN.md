# Monetisation plan

The mission is participation first. Commercial placements should fund the site without turning it into noise.

## Phase 1: POC

- Show empty sponsor slots.
- Do not run third-party ad scripts yet.
- Build traffic through useful official links and major tournament search.
- Keep logs/analytics privacy-conscious.

## Phase 2: Local and foundation sponsorships

Possible packages:

- Foundation sponsor: prominent site-wide placement, mission statement, monthly report.
- Community sponsor: smaller placements for local businesses and clubs.
- Sport pathway sponsor: sponsor a curated sport guide, clearly labelled.
- Accessibility sponsor: sponsor inclusive-sport resources or guides.

## Phase 3: Carefully labelled ads / referrals

Only after a sponsor policy exists:

- affiliate links for sporting equipment where the relationship is disclosed,
- ticket/hospitality referrals where event terms allow it,
- display ads with category exclusions,
- newsletter sponsorships if a newsletter is added.

## Red lines

Reject advertising that is likely to harm trust or participation:

- gambling-first campaigns,
- misleading performance supplements,
- exploitative financial products,
- adult content,
- discriminatory messaging,
- anything that implies a sporting body has endorsed the site when it has not.
