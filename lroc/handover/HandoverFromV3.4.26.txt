LROC Handover From v3.4.26 — Phantom Page Centreline Trim

Current baseline: v3.4.26.

Focus of this release:
- The persistent phantom/ghost final article page is addressed at the clipped-window measurement layer.
- The A4 preview and Issue Builder now use centre-point rect counting for text/media page-window stats.
- This avoids treating a tiny edge intersection from a glyph/line/figure as content on the next article page.
- The A4 preview also exposes renderer page-count diagnostics in a collapsible details block, so if the ghost remains the page stats can be read directly from the UI.

Preserved behaviours:
- Caption double-click editing.
- Advert insertion without captions.
- Manual image earlier/later controls.
- Responsive Refresh A4 preview button.
- Article placement status sync.
- Article-chain locking.
- LROC footer style.
- Deploy helper v1.13 in lroc/deployer/deploy_helper.py.

Testing notes:
- Open/save the article once after deploy.
- Refresh the A4 preview and inspect the page count.
- Re-place the article fresh into Issue Builder so old generated page metadata is not reused.
- If a phantom page remains, expand the A4 preview renderer diagnostics and capture the per-page stats for the final page.
