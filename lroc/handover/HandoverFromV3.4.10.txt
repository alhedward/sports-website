LROC handover from v3.4.10 — Article Renderer Column Reset
Timestamp: 2026-06-14 15:29:03 AEST

Baseline:
- v3.4.8 integrated the deploy helper under lroc/deployer.
- v3.4.9 attempted no-blank behaviour with explicit columns, but testing showed bullets still missing and whitespace worse.

Important lesson:
- Do not keep building on the v3.4.7/v3.4.9 destructive block packer.
- Splitting list HTML and rebuilding explicit columns is too fragile for TinyMCE article content.
- The target article body flow is represented by the user's td5_gearbox_article_A4_2column_no_blank PDF: dense two-column body flow, visible bullets, images allowed to scale/move, and LROC's own footer retained.

v3.4.10 approach:
- Preserve original TinyMCE HTML rather than splitting it into one-off list/body fragments.
- Generate issue pages as clipped windows across a native CSS multi-column article sheet.
- Use scroll-width/heuristic estimation to create the needed number of issue pages.
- Store full article HTML in each article page slot, with mcePageOffsetXPx selecting the visible window.
- Add CSS max-height control for images so oversized images do not dominate a column.
