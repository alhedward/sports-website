LROC v3.4.32 — Inline Advert Fill Consistency
Timestamp: 2026-06-16 22:27:30 AEST

Continuation notes:
- This is a narrow follow-up to v3.4.31 inline advert layout modes.
- User observed TinyMCE showed the inserted advert filling the column while A4 preview / Issue Builder rendered it smaller.
- v3.4.32 makes inline advert images fill their advert figure in editor, A4 preview, measurement, and Issue Builder render paths.
- Insert advert now stores data-mag-advert-layout="full" by default; older inline adverts without a layout are normalised to full during save/preview.
- Do not apply normal article image captions or fine borders to inline adverts.
