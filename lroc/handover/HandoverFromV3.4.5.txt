LROC Handover — v3.4.5 Article Renderer Restoration
Timestamp: 2026-06-14 12:08 AEST

Current baseline:
- Version: 3.4.5
- Package purpose: restore TinyMCE article rendering in Issue Builder after v3.4.4 could blank generated article pages.

What happened:
- v3.4.3 generated multiple TinyMCE article pages but allowed the external Article Library title/header to repeat and could lose continuation-page fragments when controls were rebuilt/saved.
- v3.4.4 attempted to preserve page fragments but was too narrow: generated article pages could lose the specific fragment payload during Issue Builder preview/editor refreshes, causing blank article rendering.
- v3.4.5 carries a hidden structured MCE payload through the slot editor so the preview/save path keeps page-specific articlePageHtml plus full articleHtml and pagination geometry.

Important implementation notes:
- Generated article pages use templateId: mce_article_page and renderMode: mce_article_page.
- The Issue Builder preview branch now merges def + value before rendering MCE pages.
- The slot editor now includes a hidden JSON payload data-mag-slot-mce-payload for MCE article pages.
- magazineReadSlotLayoutFromEditor parses that hidden payload and preserves it through save/preview refresh.
- The render-mode select now includes mce_article_page explicitly.
- Whitespace-only text/header overrides are still treated as blank.
- Repeated external Article Library slot titles remain suppressed for mce_article_page.
