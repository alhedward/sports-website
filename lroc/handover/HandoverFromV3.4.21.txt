LROC handover from v3.4.21
==========================

Current status
--------------
The article renderer is using the native CSS multicolumn/windowed article path. Caption handling from v3.4.13 is working well, advert caption suppression from v3.4.20 is retained, and the Refresh A4 preview button remains responsive.

v3.4.21 specifically fixes a v3.4.20 regression where the clipped-window meaningful-page test under-read continuation pages and caused both the A4 preview and Issue Builder placement to show only page 1. The page count now uses clipped-window measurement as a trailing ghost-page trim signal, while retaining continuation pages when the block estimator plus column/content measurements agree that content continues beyond page 1.

It also removes empty inline advert figure wrappers left by TinyMCE after an inserted advert is deleted.

- Verify no phantom final page is created after moving the offending image.
