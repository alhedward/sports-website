LROC handover from v3.4.24
===========================

Version: 3.4.24 - Article Chain Lock Propagation
Timestamp: 2026-06-15 AEST

Context
-------
Tony found that Article Library placement status and Issue Builder generated pages were finally becoming aware of each other, but locking still operated page-by-page. For generated magazine articles, this allowed page 1 to be protected while its continuation pages remained overwriteable.

Changes in this package
-----------------------
- Added Issue Builder article-chain lock propagation.
- If the first page of a placed/generated article is locked, continuation pages belonging to the same article are automatically locked.
- The chain matcher recognises TinyMCE article pages, source document pages, composer pages and content items whose assignedPageNumbers link them to the current article.
- Existing locked/fixed pages, front cover and back-cover advert pages are skipped.
- Included v1.13 deploy helper quick deploy prompt from the deployer folder.

Preserved behaviour
-------------------
- Article Library page selector status sync from v3.4.23.
- Phantom-page trimming from v3.4.22.
- Insert Advert caption suppression from v3.4.20.
- Image caption double-click behaviour from v3.4.13.
- LROC footer style.

Next watch points
-----------------
- Test locking page 1 of a placed TinyMCE article. Pages 2/3/etc. should become locked automatically.
- Confirm that locking a continuation page directly does not unexpectedly lock unrelated pages.
- Confirm that unlocking page 1 does not automatically unlock continuation pages; this is intentionally conservative unless Tony asks for symmetric unlock behaviour.
- Continue testing phantom-page trim in A4 preview and Issue/Image Builder.
