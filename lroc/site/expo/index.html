What to expect

A day built around Land Rover enthusiasm

Browse club displays, talk to owners, compare builds, find parts and ideas, and enjoy a relaxed winter day at Hawkesbury Showground.

Display

Bring the passion

Defender, Discovery, Range Rover, Series, Perentie, Forward Control, restored, modified or working — the variety is the point.

Explore

Meet owners and clubs

Talk practical upgrades, touring setup, restoration work, camping ideas and the stories behind the vehicles.

Buy · Swap · Sell

Parts, ideas and gear

Find useful spares, tools, accessories and trader displays while catching up with the wider Land Rover community.
