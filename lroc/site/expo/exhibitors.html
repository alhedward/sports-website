Who should exhibit?

Owners with restored, original, modified, touring, camping, military, classic or modern vehicles are encouraged to take part. The Expo is stronger when it shows the whole Land Rover story.

  • Club members and invited owners
  • Restoration and preservation displays
  • Touring and camping setups
  • Special interest vehicles

Exhibitor info to confirm

  • Arrival and setup times
  • Gate entry instructions
  • Display area allocation
  • Insurance or safety requirements
  • Pack-down timing

Formal exhibitor application forms can be linked from Downloads when ready.
