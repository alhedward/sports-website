- 100% renders the banner backgrounds solid; lower percentages make the bar backgrounds translucent.
- Banner opacity affects the top and bottom bar background/chrome only; banner text remains fully opaque/readable.
- Existing top/bottom banner colour controls are preserved.
- Front-cover live preview, revert stack and save behaviour remain unchanged.
